About me

A short summary of what I am and what I do.

Hi, my name is Nikolay and I am a student and the Technical University of Berlin, Germany in the Bachelor course Business informatics (original name in German - Wirtschaftsinformatik).

I was born and raised in Sofia, Bulgaria and during my High School years I was introduced to programming with languages like Visual Basic and C#.

A couple of years later, in 2017, I was accepted at the Technical university of Berlin, Germany and it was during the summer of 2017 and the first year of college that I really started to focus on Software engineering and experimentation with different languages and technologies. It was during the first semester when I successfully launched my first Android app on the Google Play Store and during the second semester when I was hired as a Software Developer at Perfacct UG in Potsdam, Germany.

There have been a lot of projects I have been part during the last couple of years and most of them are documented in the Projects page on this website, so check them out, if you are interested.

I am always interested and open to new ideas, so feel free to reach out to me! We might as well create the next big thing!
